\section{HRS Detectors Safety Checklist}
Check the following items before declaring HRS detectors are safe for
operations.
\begin{itemize}
\item The LeCroy high voltage system is operational and all the cables
are connected to the detectors.
\item The VDC gas flow in turned on and flow is seen from the exhaust.
\item The power supply is turned on for the VDC amplifier cards and
the level translators, and it is operational.
\item Dry air flow is turned on for PMTs on S0, S1 and S2m
detectors.
\item Power to the front-end discriminator is turned on for s2m
detector.
\item All high voltage and signal cables are secured. There are no
dangling cables in the detector hut.
\end{\itemize}
